bucket game

setup

type the thing you want to put in the bucket and click "add thing"

please only click it once or you'll end up adding lots of the same thing

don't touch this section once the game has started!

play!

when it is your go, click 'take one' to take a thing from the bucket

be very careful! you cannot pass, so only click again if you got the last one right

if you run out of time, click 'put back' to put it back in the bucket

don't touch this section if it is not your turn!
